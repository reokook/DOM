Jungkook, whose full name is **Jeon Jung-kook**, is a South Korean singer and songwriter. He is best known as a member of the globally popular boy band **BTS**. Jungkook was born on **September 1, 1997**, in Busan, South Korea. He has also made a name for himself as a solo artist. His solo debut single, **"Seven"** featuring Latto, was released in 2023 and broke numerous records. He followed up with another hit single, **"3D"** featuring Jack Harlow, which also achieved significant success. Jungkook is known for his powerful vocals, dance skills, and charming personality.
